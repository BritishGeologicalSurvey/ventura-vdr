Systems Thinking based Causal Loop Diagram

In parallel to integrated water management simulations by the semi-distributed hydrological model, we used systems thinking to understand the challenges and opportunities of water governance and management. Results are summarised in a causal loop diagram co-developed with stakeholders from the Great Manchester Combined Authority (GMCA), Environment Agency (EA), and United Utilities (UU). We engaged these stakeholders in a series of interviews and group model building workshops throughout 2022.

The Causal Loop Diagram includes three key components:

  • The illustration of water flows and water neutrality
  • The impact of urban developments and climate change, and risks of system failures
  • The illustration of why digital tools (such as VDRs) can support a shift to better decision-making on water governance and management

Overall, the causal loop diagram shows how the Virtual Decision Room (VDR) can best support the decision-making process. In the following slides, we unfold the model and walk you through its components and resulting systems thinking insights.
